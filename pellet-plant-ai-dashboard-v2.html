Key Chemical Reactions — Induration Furnace
DRYING · 200–400°C
Fe₂O₃·H₂O → Fe₂O₃ + H₂O↑
Remove free + capillary moisture. Ramp <50°C/min avoids thermal cracking
PREHEATING · 800–1100°C
3Fe₂O₃ → 2Fe₃O₄ + ½O₂
Oxidation front must reach pellet core before firing zone
FIRING · 1260–1340°C
Fe₂O₃ → Hematite sintering → Ceramic bonds
Recrystallization drives CCS. Target temp ±8°C with APC
AFTER-FIRING · 900–1100°C
Fe₃O₄ + O₂ → Fe₂O₃ (re-oxidation)
Incomplete oxidation → low CCS, swelling in blast furnace
