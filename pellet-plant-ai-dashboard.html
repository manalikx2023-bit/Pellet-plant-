Key Chemical Reactions — Induration
DRYING ZONE · 200–400°C
Fe₂O₃·H₂O → Fe₂O₃ + H₂O↑
Remove free + capillary moisture. Critical: avoid thermal shock >50°C/min
PREHEATING · 800–1100°C
3Fe₂O₃ → 2Fe₃O₄ + ½O₂
Oxidation of magnetite. Core must be fully oxidized before firing
FIRING · 1260–1340°C
Fe₂O₃ → Hematite sintering → Ceramic bonds
Recrystallization forms CCS-determining ceramic bridges
AFTER-FIRING · 900–1100°C
Fe₃O₄ + O₂ → Fe₂O₃ (re-oxidation)
Complete oxidation. Incomplete → low CCS, BF swelling
